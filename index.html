Introduction

I created this web page as the capstone project of the Coursera Web Design for Everybody (Basics of Web Development and Coding) Specialization. This page is meant to tell you about my experiences while trying to find a work setup that suits my style. Some time ago, I began to feel pain on the outside of my left wrist. It wasn't RSI (repetitive strain injury) or anything like that but it was alarming nonetheless. This is why I went online to do some research as to what might be causing the pain as well as how I could avoid it possibly turning into something more serious. This page contains information about what tools I use to work digitally and in the analog world as well as some explanation as to what my thought process has been to arrive at my final setup.
I hope that my experiences on the subject might allow someone to get inspiration on how to deal with creating an ergonomic workspace.

DISCLAIMER: I am not a physician or expert on this topic; what works for me does not have to work for you. Listen to your body!
